Introducing Ludwig Ro Bothoven.

2021 - 2 Months - Solo

Tasked with creating an entertaining cardboard robot - I concieved Ludwig Ro Bothoven. PS3 controllers connect via bluetooth to an ESP32 and an Arduino Mega 2560. These move two robotic hands up and down the piano, play with their 8 hammers, and perform preprogrammed demos.
