Circular design with Soap Bros.

2020 - 3 Months - Group

In 5 years 2 in 3 of us will live in water-stressed areas. After analysising the sustainability of soap, it was clear going plastic free wasn't enough. SOAP BROS also reduces water consumption by 65% in hand washing.

Sustainability

Using OnShape, mates and motion links simulated the product mechansim - validated with 3D printing, wooden prototyping. A lathe was then used to create the final parts.

01

Packaging Design

Creating a substantial locking mechanism in such a small area creating technical challenges. Using steel pins to interlock with solid aluminium hinges, the arms' rotation can be limited.

02

System Design

A lathe was used to bore, tap, chamfer and face the body. A custom jig was used to form the bar frame. Parts were tapped, welded and filed to finish this pre-production prototype.

02

Disruption

Anthropometric data guided them dimensioning. A spring loaded popper mechaism, implanted within the carbon fibre tubing, makes the design suitable for the 5th to 95th height percentile.
