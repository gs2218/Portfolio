Circular design with Soap Bros.

2020 - 3 Months - Group

In 5 years 2 in 3 of us will live in water-stressed areas. After analysising the sustainability of soap, it was clear going plastic free wasn't enough. SOAP BROS also reduces water consumption by 65% in hand washing.

*For all the project aspects discussed I was the primary or sole contributor.



Disruptive Thinking

Bold, ambitious drive combined with detailed, challenging conversations enabled a product which can to create real behavioural change.

Prototyping

As 'head chef', I made the balls using a gastronomy technique called reverse spherification. This traps optimised water and soap volumes inside algal jelly.

Packaging Design

The secondary packaging stores 20 columns of 20 orbs per stackable container. The single mold design prevents high pressure popping the orbs and allows each column to be distributed independently.

System Design

Ensuring sustainability at each stage of the process required system thinking. This design works with Acado deliveries for refill and recovery combined with long-life, entirely recyclable packaging.

*Graphic by Martin Lombard

Sustainability

Saving 985ml of water per handwash saves 540kg CO2 pp per year. 150 orbs can be produced for £1.13 and retail at a price of £3 (comparable to bar and liquid soap per wash).
