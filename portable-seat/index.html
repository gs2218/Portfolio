Portable seat for commuters.

2017 - 9 Months - Solo

This portable seat slots into a briefcase or backpack allowing commuters to always have a seat when travelling on public transport.




CAD

The required foldability was modelled using OnShape. 3D printed and wooden prototypes then validated the mecahnism.

Human Centered

A spring loaded popper mechanism allows the design to cater to the 5th to 95th height percentiles.

Mechanism Design

Using steel pins to interlock with solid aluminium hinges, a high strength mechansim could be created in a small space.

Metal Working

A lathe work, jig construction and welding contributed to creating this pre-production prototype.
