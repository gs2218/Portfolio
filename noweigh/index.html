No Weight ! Authomatic Scale

2021 - 3 Months - Group

A market ready automatic scale designed to help those with low motor skills. Branded for OXO.

*For all the project aspects discussed I was the primary or sole contributor.



Sketching

Balancing OXO’s brand image and our user feedback rough sketches developed into the final form.

FEA

FEA was informed many design decisions. Here it help to optimise the internal mechanism.

DFM

Once the split lines were assigned, draft and thickness analysis were performed.

Costing

Using Solidworks costing tool a BOM was created through assigning a material and manufacturing process to each part.

Technical Drawings

Finally, a production package was produced consisting of 4 assemblies and more than 30 drawings.



Thanks to my group members: Cayla Barker, Freya Smith and Ben Stevenson.
